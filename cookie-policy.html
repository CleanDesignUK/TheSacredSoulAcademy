Cookie Policy

Last updated: 01 January 2025

This Cookie Policy explains what cookies are, how we use them, and how you can manage or remove them.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences, personalize your experience, and provide analytical data to website operators.

How We Use Cookies

  • Essential Cookies: These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly. Without them, certain services like user logins or shopping carts may not operate.
  • Preference Cookies: We use these to remember your preferences and settings, improving your browsing experience.
  • Analytics Cookies: We may use cookies such as Google Analytics to gather usage data, such as which pages are visited, how long users spend on pages, and link clicks. This data helps us improve our services.
  • Third-Party Cookies: Some embedded content (e.g., videos, maps, or social media widgets) may set their own cookies. We do not control these cookies.

Managing Cookies

When you first visit our site, you can choose to accept or decline cookies using our cookie banner. If you accept, we store a small record so we don't prompt you repeatedly.

You can also adjust your browser settings to block or delete cookies. Please note that disabling cookies might limit certain functionalities of our website.

Changes to This Policy

We may update our Cookie Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated “Last updated” date.
