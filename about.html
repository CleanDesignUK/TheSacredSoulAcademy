Who I Am

Hello, I’m Mikey, an Indigo Child with a deep connection to the spiritual realm. From a young age, I was drawn to the mysteries of energy and intuition—acquiring my first deck of oracle cards at just 11 years old. My journey has been one of continuous exploration and growth, enriched by over 25 years of dedicated study with some of the most esteemed spiritual teachers around the world.

As a Master Teacher in Usui Reiki, Angelic Reiki, Intuitive Crystal Healing, and Angel Intuition, I embrace a variety of modalities that resonate deeply with my spirit. My passion is not only to facilitate healing for my clients and students but also to empower them to live authentically.

I work closely with my team of spirit guides and celestial beings, with the enlightening presence of Archangel Metatron guiding my path. This collaboration allows me to offer tailored services that nurture personal growth and healing.

My Mission & Vision

At The Sacred Soul Academy, my mission is to create a sacred space where you can explore and integrate healing modalities that resonate with your soul. I believe in the transformative power of spiritual, emotional, and energetic healing in fostering personal growth and inner peace.

I envision a community where every individual is supported in their unique spiritual journey—where healing is accessible, and the deep connection between mind, body, and spirit is honored.

My Philosophy

I believe that true healing is a holistic process that embraces the mind, body, and spirit as interconnected parts of a whole. My approach combines a variety of healing techniques, thoughtfully chosen to address each aspect of your being, with the aim of helping you release emotional blocks, restore your energy balance, and cultivate a deep sense of inner peace.

At the heart of my practice are compassion, authenticity, and respect. I ensure that each client and student feels supported and heard, creating a safe environment to explore your unique spiritual path, gain clarity, and embrace personal growth.

I understand that everyone’s journey is different, which is why I offer a variety of approaches—from hands-on healing and guided meditation to spiritual coaching. For those who find group sessions challenging, I also offer personalized 1-1 sessions.

Above all, my philosophy is about honoring your unique journey and helping you align with the energies that support your growth and healing. I’m here to guide you as you connect with your inner wisdom and step into your most authentic, empowered self.

My Journey

Growing up in the spiritually rich landscape of Pembrokeshire, I was drawn to the unseen energies that permeate our world from a young age. Surrounded by the natural beauty of this sacred land, I found myself deeply connected to the healing power of crystals and the wisdom of oracle cards. However, it was the loss of my paternal grandmother at 16 that truly set me on my spiritual path. Her passing led me to Spiritualism, and through attending Spiritualist Churches, I discovered the profound power of energy, hands-on healing, mediumship, Reiki, and other transformative modalities. This experience opened a doorway to a deeper connection with the spiritual realm and ignited my soul’s journey.

As I continued to explore, I followed the whispers of my heart, diving into all aspects of spirituality that resonated with me. I firmly believe that everything is energy, and as we evolve individually and collectively, it becomes essential to work with energies that align with our true essence. This understanding has become the cornerstone of my practice, enabling me to help others navigate their spiritual journeys with clarity and compassion.

Alongside my spiritual exploration, my love for animals has been lifelong. From childhood, I felt a profound bond with the animal kingdom—this connection has shaped my life, and my dedication to animal rescue continues to amplify my soul’s growth and purpose.

My quest for spiritual knowledge has led me to study with some of the world’s most esteemed spiritual teachers and visit deeply sacred places across the globe, absorbing their spiritual wisdom. I was honoured to receive blessings and teachings from Buddhist and Taoist Monks—experiences that enriched my spiritual understanding and deepened my connection to the divine.

Each step of my journey has brought me closer to the wisdom and practices that I now share with my clients and students. With a heart dedicated to healing, I aim to help you connect with your inner wisdom, align with your true purpose, and embrace the energies that support your personal growth.
