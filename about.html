Who I Am

Hello, I’m Mikey, an Indigo Child with a deep connection to the spiritual realm. From a young age, I have been drawn to the mysteries of energy and intuition, acquiring my first deck of oracle cards at just 11 years old. My journey has been one of continuous exploration and growth, enriched by over 25 years of dedicated study with some of the most esteemed spiritual teachers across the globe.

As a Master Teacher in Usui Reiki, Angelic Reiki, Intuitive Crystal Healing, and Angel Intuition, I have embraced a variety of modalities that resonate deeply with my spirit. My passion lies in not only facilitating healing for my clients and students but also in empowering them to embrace lives that are true to their authentic selves.

I work closely with my team of spirit guides and celestial beings, with the enlightening presence of Archangel Metatron guiding my path. This collaborative approach allows me to offer a tailored service that meets the unique needs of each individual. My aim is to create a nurturing environment where healing and personal growth can flourish.
