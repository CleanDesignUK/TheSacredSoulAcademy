Reiki Drum Practitioner Course

The combination of Reiki energy and rhythmic drumming creates a deeply transformative and healing experience. This two-day Reiki Drum Course introduces you to the profound practice of Reiki Drumming—a technique that merges Reiki’s high vibrational energy with the sacred power of sound healing. By integrating drumming into your Reiki practice, you can enhance healing sessions, deepen meditations, and offer a new dimension of therapeutic work to your clients and students.

This course is designed for Reiki practitioners looking to expand their healing techniques and bring a new dynamic to their spiritual practice.

Course Details & Investment

Duration: 2 Full Days
Investment: £250
Prerequisite: Reiki Level Two (Usui Reiki Second Degree Certification)

What You Will Learn & Experience

  • Preparing Yourself for Reiki Drumming – Aligning your energy before working with the drum.
  • Creating a Sacred Healing Space – Setting up an environment that enhances spiritual work.
  • Purification & Smudging Rituals – Using cleansing techniques for energetic clarity.
  • Honoring the Seven Sacred Directions – A guided meditation ceremony to align with universal energies.
  • Introduction to Reiki Drumming – Understanding the origins, techniques, and healing potential.
  • Scientific Insights into Drumming & Healing – Exploring research on the therapeutic effects of rhythm.
  • Reiki Drum Journey – Finding Your Voice – Connecting with your inner rhythm and self-expression.
  • Drum Selection Ceremony – Understanding your drum’s energy and attuning to its vibration.
  • Using Reiki Symbols with Drumming – Integrating sacred symbols into your drum practice.
  • Manifestation & Distance Healing with Drumming – Harnessing rhythmic intention for transformation.
  • Group Drumming for Collective Healing – Experiencing the power of group resonance.
  • Reiki Drum Attunement – Receiving a sacred energetic connection to your drum.
  • Meeting Your Power Animal – A guided journey to connect with your spiritual ally.
  • Communing with the Healing Spirit of Your Drum – Deepening your bond with this sacred instrument.
  • Reiki Drum Healing Techniques – Giving and receiving energy through drum vibration.
  • Mental & Emotional Reprogramming with Drumming – Clearing subconscious blocks through sound and intention.
  • Reiki Drum Journey Work – Exploring transformative rhythmic meditations.
  • Ethics & Professional Standards for Practitioners – Understanding your responsibilities as a Reiki Drum healer.
  • Closing Ceremony – Sealing the energy and honoring the journey.
