Angel Card Readings

Gentle Guidance from the Angelic Realms & Beyond

Angel Card Readings offer loving, intuitive guidance from the angelic realms, as well as support from spirit guides, deities, and other benevolent beings. These sessions are designed to uplift, bring clarity, and help you navigate life with greater confidence and peace.

Whether you're facing a challenge, seeking reassurance, or simply looking to reconnect with your inner wisdom, an Angel Card Reading provides insight into your current path and the energies surrounding you. Messages are shared in a calm, supportive space and may include intuitive interpretation, channeled guidance, and light energy work when guided.

Rather than predicting set outcomes, these readings empower your free will and offer spiritual tools to help you move forward with trust. Many find them especially healing during times of transition, uncertainty, or emotional overwhelm.

If Angel Cards don’t resonate with you, traditional Tarot cards can be used to complete your reading—ensuring the experience aligns with your personal comfort and spiritual connection.

Available in person or remotely, with recorded sessions delivered within 48 hours.

Reiki Drum Technique

Where rhythmic drumming meets Reiki’s tranquil embrace.

An Immersive Rhythmic Journey

Reiki Drum Treatment is a powerful fusion of Reiki energy healing and the rhythmic pulse of drumming, designed to bring deep relaxation, emotional release, and energetic alignment. This unique therapy uses the healing vibration of sound alongside universal life force energy to support profound inner transformation.

During a one-hour session (with approximately 45 minutes of active treatment), you remain fully clothed and comfortably lie down while the practitioner combines the steady beat of the drum with gentle Reiki energy. The rhythmic sound waves help to shift stagnant or blocked energy, encourage natural detoxification, and guide you into a deeply meditative state.

This treatment can support a wide range of healing needs. Many people experience a noticeable reduction in stress and anxiety, a release of emotional tension, and an increased sense of calm and clarity. It can also improve mental focus, enhance sleep quality, and restore a sense of energetic balance throughout the body. For those on a spiritual journey, Reiki Drum can strengthen inner connection and promote a deep sense of peace and presence.

The union of rhythm and Reiki creates a nurturing space for healing on all levels—physical, emotional, mental, and spiritual—helping you to reconnect with yourself and return to a state of natural harmony.

Investment is £33.00 for one hour treatment and £44 for 90mins, with the first session being slightly longer to include a consultation.

Usui Reiki

Reiki is a soothing, non-invasive energy healing technique that helps promote balance and well-being across the body, mind, and spirit. Through the gentle channeling of universal life force energy, Reiki supports your body’s natural healing abilities.

During a session, you remain fully clothed and either lie down or sit comfortably. The treatment can be hands-on with light touch, or hands-off, where the practitioner works just above your body’s energy field—whichever feels most comfortable for you. Reiki promotes deep relaxation, reduces stress and anxiety, eases emotional tension, and supports recovery from illness or fatigue.

In addition to in-person treatments, distant Reiki healing is available, allowing you to receive healing no matter where you are in the world.

Whether you're looking for relaxation, emotional release, or energetic renewal, Reiki offers a gentle and effective healing experience.
