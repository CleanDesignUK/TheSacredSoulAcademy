Reiki Drum Treatment

Where rhythmic drumming meets Reiki’s tranquil embrace.

An Immersive Rhythmic Journey

Reiki Drum Treatment combines the healing power of Reiki with the rhythmic vibrations of a drum to promote deep relaxation, emotional release, and energetic balance. The sound waves help shift stagnant energy, clear blockages, and bring harmony to mind, body, and spirit.

During a one-hour session (about 45 minutes of active treatment), you remain fully clothed and usually lie down while the practitioner uses drumming and Reiki to create a deeply meditative healing experience. This union of rhythm and Reiki can restore inner peace and well-being.
